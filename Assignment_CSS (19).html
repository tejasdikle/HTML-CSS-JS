HTML5 is Web platform. It is collection of technologies. It is from WHATWG and W3C.

The aside element is new to HTML5 and it can be used in two different contexts. Basically, the context of the aside element is based on whether or not it inside or outside the article element.

The HTML article tag is used to represent an article. More specifically, the content within the article tag is independent from the other content on the site.This could be a forum post, a magazine or newspaper article, a blog entry, a user-submitted comment, an interactive widget or gadget, or any other independent item of content..
